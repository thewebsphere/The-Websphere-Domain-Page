What is Software Engineering?

Web development is the art and science of building and maintaining websites. It involves designing, coding, testing, and deploying web applications that are responsive, accessible, and user-friendly.

Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC)

The SDLC is a systematic process used to build quality software in a cost-effective and efficient manner. It includes: Requirements Gathering – Identifying user needs and business goals. Analysis – Evaluating feasibility and planning the project. Design – Creating wireframes, mockups, and architectural plans. Development – Writing clean, efficient code. Testing – Rigorous quality assurance to ensure functionality. Deployment – Launching the application to production. Maintenance – Ongoing support and updates.
